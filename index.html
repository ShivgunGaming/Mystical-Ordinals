Welcome to the Mystical Ordinals!

Welcome to our exclusive Ordinals collection, The Mystical Ordinals! We use a unique style of art called Irezumi, which is a Japanese art style that originated centuries ago by the Horishi!
We aim to transend time by preserving the art of Irezumi on the Bitcoin Blockchain forever!
